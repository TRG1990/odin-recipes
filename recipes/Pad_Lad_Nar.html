Pad Lad Nar

Description

Chewy wide rice noodles are drenched in a luscious pork gravy; it's the "comfort food" of Thai street food. It's simple and quick to make, but the quality of ingredients are important, so some attention is required.

Ingredients (Serves 4)

  • 10oz wide rice noodles
  • 4tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1tbsp black soy sauce
  • 1/2lb chicken, cut into thin slices
  • 3tbsp tapioca flour
  • 1tbsp Maggi seasoning
  • 1.5 cups broccoli
  • 1tbsp coarsely chopped garlic
  • 1tbsp yellow bean sauce
  • 3 cups chicken stock
  • 1tbsp fish sauce
  • 1tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1.5tbsp sugar (or to taste)
  • .5tsp Thai pepper powder

Steps

  1. Soak the rice noodles in warm water for 30min, then rinse in cold water
  2. Add the noodles to a pot of boiling water for 1min. Remove and rinse in cold water again, and set aside
  3. Toss the sliced chicken with the Maggi seasonsing and 1tbsp of tapioca flour. Marinate for 10min
  4. Separate the noodles in a mixing bowl and toss thoroughly with the black soy sauce and 1tbsp vegetable oil
  5. Fry the noodles in a nonstick pan until golden brown. Remove from pan and keep warm
  6. Saute the garlic in oil over medium heat until browned. Add the yellow bean sauce and stir until fragrant
  7. Add the chicken to the garlic and yellow bean sauce and saute until cooked. Add 2.5 cups of chicken stock
  8. When stock begins to boil, add broccoli and season with fish sauce, oyster sauce, and sugar. Stir well.
  9. Mix the remaining .5 cup of chicken stock with the remaining tapioca flour, then stir in with the chicken and broccoli until it thickens. Remove from heat
  10. Arrange the fried noodles on a serving plate, and spoon the gravy over the noodles. Sprinkle with Thai pepper powder, and serve
